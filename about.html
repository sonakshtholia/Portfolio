About Me

Who I Am

I'm Sonaksh Tholia, a multidisciplinary artist and designerdriven by an insatiable curiosity for visual storytelling and experimental expression. My creative practice spans across various mediums, with a strong focus on crafting immersive and emotionally resonant visuals. Rooted in a love for bold aesthetics, my work often explores maximalist and grungy sensibilities—embracing chaos, texture, and imperfection as essential tools for communication.

Ever since childhood, I’ve been deeply immersed in the world of comics, cartoons, and animated storytelling. Growing up on gems like Chacha Chaudhary, Tinkle, and a constant stream of animated shows, I developed an early fascination with characters, narratives, and the power of visual worlds. Stories didn’t just entertain me—they shaped the way I saw and interpreted the world. That love for storytelling continues to fuel my creative journey today. Whether I’m designing, illustrating, or experimenting with visuals, I always try to approach my work with a sense of wonder and originality. My aim is to offer viewers a fresh, unexpected perspective—something that feels personal yet universally engaging. I see every project as a chance to build a moment of connection, to surprise, to evoke emotion, and to tell a story that lingers.

When I’m not stuck to my laptop screen, buried in layers on Photoshop, I’m usually recharging in quieter ways—flipping through pages of Stoic philosophy, sketching freely with my trusty gel pen, or chasing some new skill or idea that’s caught my curiosity. I like keeping my mind in motion, whether it’s through creating, reflecting, or just exploring something I don’t know yet. That balance between chaos and stillness keeps me grounded—and keeps the work interesting.
